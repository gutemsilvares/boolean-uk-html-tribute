Britain Tribute to NHS Workers

We’d like to give a big thanks to all our colleagues, working tirelessly on the frontline to care for people in need, and our support staff working behind the scenes, in the fight against coronavirus.

We’re truly inspired by your personal commitment to making a difference in these unprecedented and challenging times. Your approach and finding ways to address issues, to do things differently, and make continuous improvement. This includes coming up with practical tools and techniques to make things work better for colleagues and most importantly the people who use our services.

Coronavirus cases in the UK are now highest among 10 to 19 year olds, according to new data. For 10 to 19-year-olds, the rate stood at 100.7 cases per 100,000 in the week to March 21, the highest rate of any age group and up by just over 20 per cent week-on-week.
